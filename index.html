What We Offer

Our Services

We focus on original art, privacy, and a premium client experience from first enquiry through to final result.

Private Studio Sessions

One-on-one sessions at our Parnell private studio. Ideal for complex, large, or intimate pieces where you need full concentration and comfort.

Door-to-Door

We bring the studio to you. Our artists travel across the Auckland region with full professional equipment. Travel deposit applies.

Design and Consultation

All designs are fully original. References are welcome — we adapt your ideas into unique, non-infringing compositions tailored to your vision.
