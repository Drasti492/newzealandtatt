Important Information

Deposit and Cancellation Policy

Deposit Policy

All bookings require a non-refundable deposit of NZD 100 to 300 depending on the session type and piece size. Your deposit is deducted from the final price at the end of your session. Deposits must be received within 48 hours of booking to hold your appointment.

Cancellations and Rescheduling

We require a minimum of 48 hours notice to reschedule your appointment. Up to two reschedules are permitted with adequate notice. Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice or no-shows will forfeit the deposit. We appreciate your understanding as last-minute changes affect our artists' income.

Payment Options

We accept payment by bank transfer, cash, and major debit cards. Deposits are paid via bank transfer once confirmed over WhatsApp. Full upfront payment is available for all session types and earns priority scheduling. Payment details are sent once your session is confirmed.

How to Book

Click any booking button on this page to open WhatsApp with your session details pre-filled. Our team responds within the hour during studio hours. You can also message us directly, browse the gallery and book from a specific tattoo card, or call in to enquire about walk-in availability.
